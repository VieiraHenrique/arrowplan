SysPat

Our Intellectual Property Valorization Platform

SysPat is a system for the Protection and the Valorization (marketing and negotiation) of Intellectual Property, whether they are in the form of Patents, Scientific Papers or Handouts (copyright).

SysPat makes easier the drafting, the filing or publishing, the management and the maximization of the value of patents, scientific papers and handouts.

About Us

Our start

Arrowplan is a company founded in 1995 in Belgium, in the heart of Europe. We started doing technology valorization in the environmental sector. And from that we expanded our technology targets, such as: security, health and space industry. Since the beginning the company was organized as a network: the Arrowplan Network. And this kind of organization, peculiar for the time it was implemented, has played a crucial role for the company business successes and for expansion of our activities.

From now on

Today we are present in several sectors like health, information technology, security, aerospace agriculture and environment, all of them strongly leveraged by our own created and developed platform SysPat, today used in several countries and that also led us to expand our work into the education arena. From there we are happy to be truly supporting the creation of an innovation culture based on Intellectual Property and on empowering creative people.
