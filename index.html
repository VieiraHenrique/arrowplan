About Us

History

Arrowplan is a company founded in 1995 in Belgium, in the heart of Europe. We started doing technology valorization in the environmental sector. And from that we expanded our technology activities to other sectors like security and Information Technology.

In year 2000 we inaugurated our second branch of activity: Arrowplan do Brasil. This move increased the collaboration projects we developed between Europe and Latin America, and started several new projects specifically constructed to the Brazilian continental market.

Organization

Since the beginning the company was organized as a network: the Arrowplan Network. And this kind of organization, unusual for the time it was implemented, has played a crucial role for the company business successes and for the expansion of our activities. The same model we have followed to develop SysPat, patent protection and valorization platform: The SysPat Network. Both Networks are today managed by Arrowplan and follow very strictly deontology principles to deal with our prime matter: Information.

From now to the Future

Today we are present in several sectors like health, information technology, security, aerospace agriculture and environment, all of them strongly leveraged by our own created and developed platform SysPat, today used in several countries and that also led us to expand our work into the education arena. From there we are happy to be truly supporting the creation of an innovation culture based on Intellectual Property and on empowering creative people.
